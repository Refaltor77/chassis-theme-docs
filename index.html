1. Installation

  1. From your Shopify admin, go to Online Store → Themes.
  2. Click Add theme and upload the theme, or install it from the Shopify Theme Store.
  3. Click Customize to open the theme editor.

3. Header & navigation

  • The main menu supports three levels. Items with children open a full-width panel on desktop and nested accordions in the mobile drawer.
  • Enable sticky header in the header section settings.
  • The announcement bar (in the header group) accepts up to three messages; extra messages are hidden on mobile.

4. Product pages

The product template is fully block-based. Reorder or remove any block: SKU, title, price, stock status, variant picker, quantity, buy buttons, pickup availability, description, specifications, collapsible rows, text — and add app blocks anywhere between them.

Specifications table

Shows native data (SKU, barcode, vendor, type, weight — each can be toggled). Add extra rows with the Specification row block; connect a row's value to a metafield with the dynamic source picker to display technical data per product.

Volume pricing & quantity rules

When a variant has quantity price breaks (B2B catalogs), the theme renders the price-break table automatically, and quantity rules (min / max / increments) drive the quantity selector.

5. Trade (B2B) pages

Create a page (Online Store → Pages), then assign the template:

TemplatePurpose
quick-orderSearch by SKU or name, build an order list, add everything to the cart at once. Customers can also paste a whole order list (SKU, quantity per line).
quoteQuote request form.
pro-accountTrade account application form.
contactContact page with form and info column.
The quote and pro-account forms are fully configurable by blocks: add fields, set their type (text, phone, number, multi-line), mark them required. Submissions arrive at your store's contact email — no app required.

6. Collection filtering

Filters use Shopify's native storefront filtering. Manage which filters appear (availability, price, product type, vendor, and any metafield) with the free Shopify Search & Discovery app. Filtering, sorting and the product count update without page reloads.

7. Recommendations

Two recommendation sections are available on product pages: related (similar products) and complementary (often bought together — configure pairs in Search & Discovery).

8. FAQ

Why does “Buy it now” keep its own colors with Shop Pay or PayPal?

Branded wallet buttons must keep their brand colors (a Shopify rule for all themes). The unbranded button follows your accent color.

Why don't my filters show product types or vendors?

Configure filter sources in the Shopify Search & Discovery app (Settings → Filters).

How do I show technical specs per product?

Create product metafields (Settings → Custom data → Products), then connect them to Specification row blocks on the product template.

Does the theme require any app?

No. Every feature above works without apps. Search & Discovery (free, by Shopify) is only needed to customize filter sources.
