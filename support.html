Support

Our commitment

We reply within two business days (Monday–Friday, CET). Critical bugs — broken checkout path, broken layout on a supported browser — are prioritized and fixed as fast as possible. Updates ship through the Shopify Theme Store with release notes.

What support covers

  • Bugs: any theme feature not working as documented on a supported browser (latest two versions of Safari/Edge, latest three of Chrome/Firefox, plus Instagram/Facebook/Pinterest webviews).
  • Help using theme settings, sections and blocks.
  • Guidance on the trade features (quick order, quote and pro-account forms, specification metafields, volume pricing display).

Not covered: custom code changes and their side effects (we recommend hiring a Shopify Partner), third-party app conflicts (we help identify them; fixing lies with the app vendor), data entry or store configuration services.
